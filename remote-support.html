Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our remote support service

Is remote support secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use AnyDesk, which employs military-grade TLS 1.2 encryption and RSA 2048 asymmetric key exchange. All connections are secure and encrypted end-to-end. You maintain full control and can disconnect at any time.

Can you really install software and make system changes remotely?

Yes! Unlike Windows Quick Assist or Chrome Remote Desktop, AnyDesk provides full administrative access with UAC elevation. This means we can install software, update drivers, modify system settings, and perform complete repairs just as if we were sitting at your computer.

What if AnyDesk won't connect or download?

If you have trouble with AnyDesk, we can guide you through alternative connection methods or provide phone-based troubleshooting. In some cases, we may recommend an on-site visit if remote connection isn't possible.

How long do remote sessions typically take?

Most remote sessions take 1-2 hours, depending on the complexity of the issue. Simple problems like software installation or virus removal often take less than an hour, while comprehensive system optimization may take longer.

Do I need to stay at my computer during the session?

For most of the session, you can step away while we work. However, we may need you available for initial setup, to approve certain changes, or to test functionality at the end of the session.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and e-transfer. Payment is due upon completion of the remote session. We'll provide a detailed invoice of all work performed.
