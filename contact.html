Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to service calls?

For standard service calls, we typically schedule within 24-48 hours. Same-day service is available for urgent issues, and emergency calls are responded to within 4 hours.

Do you provide on-site service?

Yes! We provide on-site service throughout the Greater Toronto Area. There's a $50 travel fee within 25km, and $2.50 per km beyond that. Many issues can be resolved right at your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, all major credit cards, PayPal, and Venmo. We offer a 5% discount for cash payments and payment plans for services over $500.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Absolutely! All labor comes with a 30-day warranty, and parts carry the manufacturer's warranty. We also offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all our work.

Can you help with both Windows and Mac computers?

Yes, we service both Windows PCs and Mac computers, as well as laptops, desktops, and servers. Our expertise covers all major operating systems and hardware platforms.

What should I do to prepare for a service visit?

Please back up any critical data if possible, have your computer accessible, and be prepared to provide any passwords needed. We'll handle the rest!
